My favorite recepie :

Aim:

To prepare maggi.

Ingredients required:

  1. 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  2. 1 1/2 cups of water
  3. Maggi tastemaker masala (flavor packet)

Procedure:

  1. Pour 1 1/2 cups of water into a pot and bring it to a boil.
  2. Break the Maggi noodles into 4 parts and add them to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the noodles for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are soft and fully cooked.
  4. Turn off the heat and add the Maggi tastemaker masala (flavor packet) to the pot.
  5. Stir the noodles and masala together until the masala is evenly distributed.
  6. Let the noodles sit for a minute or two to absorb the flavor
  7. Serve the Maggi noodles hot and enjoy!
